Aim for clarity

  • Provide context in titles and subtitles
  • Remove redundant elements (e.g. axis titles, legends, etc.)
  • Annotate critical points of data
  • Use conventional abbreviations for labels
  • Keep text horizontal
  • Increase density of meaningful information

Consider D3.js

What D3.js offers

  • Mix of low-level modules
  • Provides flexibility to compose visualizations
  • Everything is customizable and interchangeable
  • Straightforward debugging
  • If you know SVG, CSS, HTML, and some javascript: dive in!
